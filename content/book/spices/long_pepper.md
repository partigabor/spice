# Long Pepper

Long Pepper (*Piper longum* L.) is a culinary and medicinal spice from the *Piperaceae* family,[^powo] originating in the region(s) of E. Himalaya to S. China; Indo-China.[^van_wyk_culinary_2014] It is used for its fruit, primarily for South and Souteast Asian cuisine; Ayurveda and TCM. Its aroma is described as pungent, peppery.[^ucla_medicinal_2002]
