# Star Anise

Star Anise (*Illicium verum* Hook.f.) is a culinary spice from the *Schisandraceae* family,[^powo] originating in the region(s) of SE. China; Vietnam.[^van_wyk_culinary_2014] It is used for its pericarp, primarily for meats, desserts; colic. Its aroma is described as bitter, licorice-like.[^ucla_medicinal_2002]
