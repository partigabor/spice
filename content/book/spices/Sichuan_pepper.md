# Sichuan Pepper

Sichuan Pepper (*Zanthoxylum bungeanum* Maxim.) is a culinary spice from the *Rutaceae* family,[^powo] originating in the region(s) of China.[^van_wyk_culinary_2014] It is used for its pericarp, primarily for meats; colic. Its aroma is described as woodsy, peppery.[^ucla_medicinal_2002]
