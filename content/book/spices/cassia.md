# Cassia

Cassia (*Cinnamomum cassia* (L.) J.Presl.) is a culinary and medicinal spice from the *Lauraceae* family,[^powo] originating in the region(s) of Southeast China.[^van_wyk_culinary_2014] It is used for its bark and fruit, primarily for flavor; diarrhea. Its aroma is described as pungent, cinnamon-like.[^ucla_medicinal_2002]
